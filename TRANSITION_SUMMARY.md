## üé® Recommended Approach: Path-Based URLs

### Structure:
```
opinionpoll.co.in/              ‚Üí Generic landing
opinionpoll.co.in/states        ‚Üí State selection
opinionpoll.co.in/bihar/        ‚Üí Bihar landing (existing content)
opinionpoll.co.in/bihar/vote    ‚Üí Bihar voting
opinionpoll.co.in/bihar/results ‚Üí Bihar results
opinionpoll.co.in/bihar/blog/   ‚Üí Bihar blogs
opinionpoll.co.in/maharashtra/  ‚Üí Maharashtra (new)
opinionpoll.co.in/jharkhand/    ‚Üí Jharkhand (new)
```

### Why Path-Based?
‚úÖ **Single domain** (stronger overall authority)  
‚úÖ **Easier internal linking** (cross-state)  
‚úÖ **Simpler to manage** (one SSL, one hosting)  
‚úÖ **Better for branding** (opinionpoll.co.in recognized)  
‚úÖ **Lower cost** (no subdomains needed)  

### 301 Redirects (Critical!):
```
/vote ‚Üí /bihar/vote (301)
/results ‚Üí /bihar/results (301)
/blog/bihar-* ‚Üí /bihar/blog/* (301)
```

These redirects preserve your hard-earned SEO juice!
