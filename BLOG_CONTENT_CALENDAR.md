## üìä Content Strategy

### **Quality Standards**
- ‚úÖ **Original Content**: 100% unique, no plagiarism
- ‚úÖ **Comprehensive**: 3000-3800 words per blog
- ‚úÖ **Data-Driven**: Include statistics, charts, infographics
- ‚úÖ **Bilingual**: Hindi primary, English keywords
- ‚úÖ **SEO Optimized**: Keywords, meta tags, internal linking
- ‚úÖ **Visual Rich**: Charts, graphs, maps, infographics
- ‚úÖ **Expert Analysis**: Deep insights, not surface-level

### **Visual Elements (Per Blog)**
1. **Header Image**: Custom designed for each blog (1200x630px)
2. **Infographics**: 2-3 data visualizations per blog
3. **Charts**: Bar charts, pie charts for statistics
4. **Maps**: District/constituency maps when relevant
5. **Tables**: Comparative data tables
6. **Quote Cards**: Key insights highlighted visually

### **SEO Strategy**
- **Primary Keywords**: Targeted for each blog
- **Long-tail Keywords**: 5-10 per article
- **Internal Links**: Link to 3-5 other blog posts
- **External Links**: Credible sources (ECI, Census, News)
- **Meta Description**: Compelling, keyword-rich
- **URL Slug**: Clean, descriptive, Hindi transliteration

### **Content Structure (Each Blog)**
1. **Introduction** (300 words)
   - Hook the reader
   - Preview main points
   - Include key statistics

2. **Main Content** (2200-3200 words)
   - 5-7 major sections with H2 headings
   - Sub-sections with H3 headings
   - Data visualization after every 500 words
   - Real examples and case studies

3. **Key Takeaways** (200-300 words)
   - Bullet-point summary
   - Action items for readers
   - Related article suggestions

4. **FAQ Section** (200-300 words)
   - 5-7 common questions answered
   - Schema markup for rich snippets
